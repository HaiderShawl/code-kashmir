About Us

Code Kashmir is an initiative which aims to introduce Kashmiri students to the world of technology. Having knowledge and experience in the technological world will benefit the students greatly in college and beyond. It will also give them a chance to be creative and create amazing projects of their own. In addition to creating guides for various technologies, Code Kashmir will also organize Interviews and events and create the first the Computer Science community of Kashmir where students can ask questions, collaborate on projects and more.
